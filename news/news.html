Stipendium Hungarcium Scholarship Programme 2019 - 2020

The new Stipendium Hungaricum Call for Applications for studies starting in the academic year 2019-2020.

Please find attached the Call for Application document, which is also available on the Tempus Public Foundation’s official website: www.stipendiumhungaricum.hu. 

call-for-applications-2019-2020 

The available scholarship types (full degree studies, the language of instruction is English) and study fields for Indonesian students: 
– Full degree master studies: in English language, primarily in the field of agriculture, natural sciences, economics, information technology and technical studies, arts and sport studies; 
– Full degree doctoral studies: in any field of interest in English language.
